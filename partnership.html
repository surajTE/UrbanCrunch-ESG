NATIONAL AGENCY, JOINT VENTURES AND DISTRIBUTION

We provide Commercial Agency and Joint Venture services for more flexible commercial solutions to facilitate consultancy, research and development, project management, support services and product distribution within the UAE.

This includes Engineering Classification for contracting services, Supreme Petroleum Council (SPC) Approval in the Petroleum Industry for the activity of “Onshore and Offshore Oil & Gas Field & Facilities Services” which is mandatory for all companies dealing with oil companies operating in the emirate of Abu Dhabi, whether in areas of services consultation, catering or supply of services. We also manage the process for issuance of the CNIA (Critical National Infrastructure Authority) passes to access the oil field sites, managed by CICPA (Critical Infrastructure and Coastal Protection Authority). CICPA security passes allow access to restricted ports and field sites. There are many restricted areas, which are not accessible without the correct and relevant CICPA pass. Each CICPA pass is unique and must be applied for individually. ESG handles the application process for our partners CICPA pass approvals as efficiently as possible.
